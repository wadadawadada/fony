▶ Listen Lounge Music in FONY

Lounge music is a genre characterized by its smooth, relaxing melodies and mellow beats, often played in cafes, bars, and chillout spaces.

Characteristic Instruments

Soft keyboards, smooth saxophones, gentle percussion, and ambient sounds are typical instruments of Lounge music.

Dominant Styles

The genre blends elements of jazz, chillout, easy listening, and electronic music, focusing on creating a relaxing atmosphere.
