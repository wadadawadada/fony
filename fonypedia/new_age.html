New Age

▶ Listen New Age Music in FONY

New Age music is a genre focused on creating atmospheric, relaxing, and meditative soundscapes. It often blends acoustic instruments with electronic elements to promote peace, healing, and spiritual awakening.

Characteristic Instruments

Common instruments include synthesizers, flutes, piano, acoustic guitars, harp, and various world instruments such as Tibetan bowls, used to craft soothing and ethereal sounds.

Dominant Styles

The genre encompasses ambient, meditation music, nature sounds, and instrumental compositions aimed at relaxation, yoga, and spiritual practices.
