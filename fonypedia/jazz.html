Jazz

▶ Listen Jazz Music in FONY

Jazz is a genre defined by its complex rhythms, improvisation, and expressive melodies, originating in the African American communities of the early 20th century.

Characteristic Instruments

Saxophone, trumpet, piano, double bass, and drums are core instruments creating Jazz’s distinctive sound.

Dominant Styles

Styles include swing, bebop, cool jazz, fusion, and free jazz, showcasing the genre’s wide-ranging evolution.
