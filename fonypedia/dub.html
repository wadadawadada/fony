Dub

▶ Listen Dub Music in FONY

Dub is a subgenre of reggae characterized by remixing existing recordings, heavy use of effects like reverb and delay, and an emphasis on bass and drums.

Characteristic Instruments

The genre prominently uses bass guitar, drums, and various studio effects to create spacious and echoing soundscapes.

Dominant Styles

Dub music focuses on instrumental remixing and often incorporates experimental production techniques, influencing many electronic music genres.
