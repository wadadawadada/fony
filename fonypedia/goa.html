Goa trance is a form of electronic music originating from the Goa region of India, known for its hypnotic rhythms and psychedelic sounds.

Characteristic Instruments

Synthesizers, drum machines, sequencers, and effects create the rich, layered textures characteristic of Goa trance.

Dominant Styles

Goa trance features fast tempos, complex layered melodies, and psychedelic elements that make it a unique electronic music subgenre.
