House

▶ Listen House Music in FONY

House music is a genre of electronic dance music characterized by a repetitive 4/4 beat and soulful vocals, originating in Chicago in the early 1980s.

Characteristic Instruments

Drum machines, synthesizers, samplers, and basslines form the core instruments of House music.

Dominant Styles

Styles include deep house, progressive house, tech house, and acid house, each bringing unique rhythmic and melodic elements.
