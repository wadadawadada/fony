Punk

▶ Listen Punk Music in FONY

Punk is a fast, raw, and rebellious genre that emerged in the 1970s, characterized by stripped-down instrumentation and anti-establishment themes.

Characteristic Instruments

Electric guitar, bass, drums, and raw vocal delivery define the punk sound.

Dominant Styles

Styles include hardcore punk, post-punk, pop punk, and ska punk, each with unique intensity and message.
