Industrial

▶ Listen Industrial Music in FONY

Industrial music is an experimental genre known for its harsh, mechanical sounds, and innovative use of noise and electronics.

Characteristic Instruments

Synthesizers, samplers, drum machines, distorted guitars, and unconventional sound sources create Industrial’s aggressive sound.

Dominant Styles

The genre includes substyles like industrial rock, EBM (Electronic Body Music), noise, and power electronics, emphasizing experimentation.
