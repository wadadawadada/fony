Rap

▶ Listen Rap Music in FONY

Rap is a vocal style within hip hop characterized by rhythmic and rhyming speech, often addressing social issues and storytelling.

Characteristic Instruments

Drum machines, samplers, turntables, and synthesizers provide the beats and backing tracks in rap music.

Dominant Styles

Styles include old school, gangsta rap, trap, conscious rap, and alternative rap, each with distinctive lyrical and rhythmic approaches.
