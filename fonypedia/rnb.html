RnB

▶ Listen RnB Music in FONY

Rhythm and Blues (RnB) is a genre blending soulful vocals, smooth melodies, and strong beats, evolving from traditional blues and jazz roots.

Characteristic Instruments

Piano, guitar, bass, drums, and keyboards shape the soulful sound of RnB music.

Dominant Styles

Contemporary RnB, neo-soul, and quiet storm are popular styles within the genre, each with emotional and smooth vibes.
