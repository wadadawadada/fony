Drum & Bass

▶ Listen Drum & Bass Music in FONY

Drum & Bass is a fast-paced electronic music genre known for its heavy basslines and rapid breakbeats, combining energetic rhythms with complex sound design.

Characteristic Instruments

Synthesizers, samplers, heavy bass, and drum machines are central to Drum & Bass production, creating its signature driving beats and basslines.

Dominant Styles

The genre includes substyles such as Liquid DnB, Neurofunk, Jump-Up, and Darkstep, each with distinct rhythms and moods.
