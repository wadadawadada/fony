Funk

▶ Listen Funk Music in FONY

Funk music is characterized by strong rhythmic grooves, prominent basslines, and an infectious danceable feel that originated in the 1960s and 1970s.

Characteristic Instruments

Bass guitar, electric guitar, drums, horns, and keyboards are central to funk’s signature sound.

Dominant Styles

Funk incorporates elements of soul, jazz, and R&B, often featuring syncopated rhythms and tight arrangements.
