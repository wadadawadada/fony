Hip Hop

▶ Listen Hip Hop Music in FONY

Hip Hop is a vibrant music genre that features rhythmic speech, DJing, sampling, and beats that have profoundly influenced global culture.

Characteristic Instruments

Turntables, samplers, drum machines, synthesizers, and vocals are key elements in Hip Hop music.

Dominant Styles

Hip Hop includes styles such as old school, gangsta rap, trap, and conscious rap, each with unique rhythms and lyrical themes.
