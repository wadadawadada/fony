Techno

▶ Listen Techno Music in FONY

Techno is a form of electronic dance music featuring repetitive beats and synthesized sounds, developed in Detroit during the 1980s.

Characteristic Instruments

Drum machines, synthesizers, sequencers, and samplers are key instruments used in techno production.

Dominant Styles

Styles include Detroit techno, minimal techno, acid techno, and tech house, each offering unique sounds and rhythms.
