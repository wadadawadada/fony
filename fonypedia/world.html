▶ Listen World Music in FONY

World music is an umbrella term for diverse traditional and folk music styles from cultures around the globe, celebrating rich heritage and cultural expressions.

Characteristic Instruments

Instruments vary widely and include sitar, didgeridoo, marimba, bagpipes, and many more depending on the region.

Dominant Styles

World music encompasses folk, indigenous, and traditional genres from Africa, Asia, Latin America, Europe, and Oceania.
