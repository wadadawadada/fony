▶ Listen Asian Music in FONY

Asian music encompasses a wide variety of traditional and contemporary styles from diverse cultures across Asia. It features unique instruments, rich melodies, and rhythmic patterns reflecting its vast heritage.

Characteristic Instruments

Traditional instruments include the erhu, shamisen, koto, tabla, and various flutes and percussion instruments, contributing to the distinct sounds of Asian music.

Dominant Styles

Dominant styles range from classical forms like Gagaku and Ragas to folk, pop, and contemporary fusion genres, showcasing Asia’s musical diversity and evolution.
