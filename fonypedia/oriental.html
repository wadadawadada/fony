Oriental

▶ Listen Oriental Music in FONY

Oriental music covers the rich and varied musical traditions of the Middle East and surrounding regions, known for its distinctive scales and rhythms.

Characteristic Instruments

Oud, qanun, ney, darbuka, and other traditional instruments create the unique sound of Oriental music.

Dominant Styles

Includes classical Arabic, Turkish, Persian music, and folk styles, with complex rhythms and modal scales.
