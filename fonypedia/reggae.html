Reggae

▶ Listen Reggae Music in FONY

Reggae is a Jamaican music genre known for its offbeat rhythms, socially conscious lyrics, and relaxed groove.

Characteristic Instruments

Guitar, bass, drums, keyboards, and horns create reggae’s distinctive rhythm and feel.

Dominant Styles

Roots reggae, dub, dancehall, and ska are important styles within the reggae tradition.
