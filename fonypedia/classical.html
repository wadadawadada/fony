Classical music is a broad tradition of Western art music, known for its rich orchestral arrangements, complex compositions, and profound influence on music history.

Characteristic Instruments

The genre features instruments such as the violin, piano, cello, flute, and clarinet, which together create the intricate textures of symphonies, sonatas, and concertos.

Dominant Styles

Classical music encompasses styles from the Baroque, Classical, Romantic, and Modern eras, each bringing unique characteristics and innovations.
