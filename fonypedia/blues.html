Blues

▶ Listen Blues Music in FONY

Blues music is a soulful and expressive genre rooted in African American history, characterized by its distinctive chord progressions and emotional depth.

Characteristic Instruments

The genre prominently features the guitar, harmonica, piano, and bass, often accompanied by drums, creating a rich and emotional sound.

Dominant Styles

Blues includes styles such as Delta Blues, Chicago Blues, and Texas Blues, each contributing unique sounds and influences to the genre’s evolution.
