Hardcore

▶ Listen Hardcore Music in FONY

Hardcore is an intense and fast subgenre of electronic music characterized by aggressive beats and high energy.

Characteristic Instruments

Drum machines, synthesizers, samplers, and heavy distortion effects are commonly used to create hardcore's powerful sound.

Dominant Styles

Styles include gabber, hard trance, and breakcore, all known for their fast tempos and energetic delivery.
