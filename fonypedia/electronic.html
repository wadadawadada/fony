Electronic

▶ Listen Electronic Music in FONY

Electronic music is a broad genre encompassing music produced using electronic instruments and technology, often characterized by synthetic sounds and innovative production techniques.

Characteristic Instruments

Synthesizers, drum machines, samplers, and computer software are fundamental tools used to create the diverse sounds within electronic music.

Dominant Styles

Electronic music includes a variety of styles such as techno, house, trance, dubstep, and ambient, each with distinct rhythms and sound textures.
